Carbohydrates

Understanding Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are one of the three macronutrients (along with proteins and fats) that provide energy to the body. They are found in a wide array of both healthy and unhealthy foods.

Types of Carbohydrates:

  • Simple Carbohydrates: These are quickly digested and provide rapid energy. Found in fruits, milk, and sugar.
  • Complex Carbohydrates: These take longer to digest and provide sustained energy. Found in whole grains, legumes, and starchy vegetables.
  • Dietary Fiber: A type of complex carb that the body can't digest, important for digestive health.

Functions in the Body:

  • Primary source of energy for the body
  • Fuel for the central nervous system
  • Help preserve muscle by preventing protein breakdown
  • Dietary fiber promotes digestive health

Best Food Sources:

Fruits: Apples, bananas, berries

Whole Grains: Brown rice, quinoa, oats

Vegetables: Sweet potatoes, peas, corn
