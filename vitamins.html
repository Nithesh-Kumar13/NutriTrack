Vitamins: Essential Micronutrients

What Are Vitamins?

Vitamins are organic compounds that our bodies need in small amounts for normal growth, development, and function. They are essential because our bodies cannot produce them (or not in sufficient quantities) and must be obtained through diet.

Types of Vitamins

Fat-Soluble Vitamins

  • Vitamin A: Vision, immune function
  • Vitamin D: Bone health, calcium absorption
  • Vitamin E: Antioxidant, skin health
  • Vitamin K: Blood clotting

Water-Soluble Vitamins

  • B-complex: Energy production, brain function
  • Vitamin C: Immune support, collagen production

Common Vitamin Deficiencies

Vitamin Deficiency Symptoms Food Sources
Vitamin D Weak bones, fatigue Fatty fish, fortified milk
Vitamin B12 Anemia, nerve damage Meat, eggs, dairy
Vitamin C Scurvy, weak immunity Citrus fruits, bell peppers
